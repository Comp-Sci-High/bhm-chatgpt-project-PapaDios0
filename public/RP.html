Rosa Parks' Achievements

  • Mother of the Civil Rights Movement
  • Refused to Give Up Her Bus Seat (1955), Sparking the Montgomery Bus Boycott
  • Key Figure in the Montgomery Bus Boycott (1955-1956)
  • Worked with Martin Luther King Jr. to Fight Segregation
  • Received the Presidential Medal of Freedom (1996)
  • Awarded the Congressional Gold Medal (1999)
  • Helped Overturn Segregation Laws in America
  • Worked for the NAACP as Secretary and Investigator
  • Author of an Autobiography: *Rosa Parks: My Story*
  • Advocated for Civil Rights Until Her Passing
  • Honored with a Statue in the U.S. Capitol
  • First Black Woman to Lie in State at the U.S. Capitol
