Martin Luther King Jr.'s Achievements

  • Leader of the Civil Rights Movement
  • Organized the Montgomery Bus Boycott (1955-1956)
  • Led the 1963 March on Washington
  • Delivered the Iconic "I Have a Dream" Speech
  • Won the Nobel Peace Prize (1964)
  • Helped Pass the Civil Rights Act of 1964
  • Instrumental in the Voting Rights Act of 1965
  • Founded the Southern Christian Leadership Conference (SCLC)
  • Advocated for Nonviolent Resistance
  • Fought for Economic Justice and Fair Wages
  • Opposed the Vietnam War with Powerful Speeches
  • Received the Presidential Medal of Freedom (Posthumously)
