This Black History Month website was created to honor and acknowledge the incredible contributions made by black individuals throughout history. By showcasing their achievements, I aim to bring awareness to the lasting impact these trailblazers have had in shaping our world. It is important to remember and celebrate the legacy of those who fought for equality, justice, and empowerment for people of color. This website serves as a tribute to their courage, perseverance, and vision for a better future.

This Website was also made to represent 5 main Black hisotrical figures that have changed hisotry for many, and it may be said above but the people mentioned in this website have changed lots of things for us such as sports, music, and our basic ways of living, so this website is to show appreciation and dedication to the work they have done, and to make work that can represent them as greatly as they have represented people of color throughout history.
